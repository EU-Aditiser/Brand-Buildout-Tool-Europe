November 2025 Patch Notes

- Updated headline processing to support both {KeyWord:Brand at iHerb} and {CUSTOMIZER.Dynamic Headline X:...} patterns.

- Implemented exact token replacement: {KeyWord:Brand at iHerb} is now replaced with {KeyWord:<brandName> at iHerb} in all Headline columns (1-15).

- CUSTOMIZER patterns and other headline values are now passed through unchanged.

- Character count for {KeyWord:...} headlines counts only the text after the colon (ignoring the closing brace).

- Headlines with CUSTOMIZER patterns and plain text are now skipped from character validation.
